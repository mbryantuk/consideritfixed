# Consider IT Fixed Pricing Strategy & Rate Card

## The "Compassionate Pricing" Strategy
Our pricing model is designed to be competitive against high-street chains while remaining deeply compassionate and accessible to the Felpham and Bognor Regis community, particularly retirees and non-technical users. 

1. **The "No Fix, No Fee" Promise:** If a problem cannot be diagnosed or resolved, the customer pays nothing. This removes financial anxiety.
2. **Capped Hourly Rates:** Labour is capped so that complex or slow tasks (like deep virus scans or data recovery) do not result in surprisingly high bills.
3. **Local Loyalty:** Call-outs within the immediate local area are completely free.
4. **Community Support:** A standard 10% discount is applied to labour for over-65s and community members on specific benefits.
